The left side is your gaming wall, dynamically scaling your covers perfectly. The right side is your control deck.

Manual & Full Scan

🧠 The Scanner Brain

ViGaVault isn't just a dumb scraper; it's highly intelligent.

  • Full Scan: Rips through your local folders and your GOG Galaxy database, pulling everything into a master list.
  • Exact Match Supremacy: When querying IGDB, the engine mathematically weighs text resemblance against IGDB Category tags (Main Game vs. DLC). It knows to prioritize the base game unless your folder explicitly asks for a "GOTY" or "Remaster"!
  • Background Magic: Downloads covers and backfills media silently so your UI never freezes.

⚙️ Settings & Rules

Tailor the engine exactly to your hard drive's layout.

  • Display: Force Dark/Light mode and slide the exact pixel sizes for your covers and text.
  • Local Copies: Use "Simple Mode" for standard folders, or switch to "Advanced" to map exactly what every single sub-folder means (e.g., tagging everything in a specific folder as "VR").
  • Media Rules: Decide whether the app should aggressively auto-download videos or just grab covers.
Settings Panel

Edit & Merge Tools

✏️ Edit & Merge (Curation)

Got a weird indie game or a stubborn ROM that scanned wrong? You have total control.

  • Manual Edits: Fix typos, assign custom local covers, or drop in a YouTube URL for a trailer.
  • Data Locking: Check the "Lock" box to protect your curated masterpiece from ever being overwritten by a future auto-scan.
  • Merging: Got duplicates? Smash them together. The Conflict Resolver lets you pick exactly which metadata fields survive the collision.

🧹 Media, Metadata & Stats

The polish phase.

Media Manager: The ultimate cleanup crew. It instantly detects any game missing a cover, trailer, or video. Just paste a direct image/video URL, and it will download it, name it, and link it perfectly without navigating a single menu.

Metadata Manager: The grand organizer. Batch-delete fragmented tags, misspelled genres, or incorrect publishers across your entire library in one click.

Statistics: Flex your numbers. See exactly how many games you own, your top developers, the oldest relic in your vault, and your exact media completion percentage!
